About..

Stack Overflow is a Q&A website for professional and enthusiast programmers. The website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions.

The members can earn "reputations points" and badges for thier valued contribution for example by answer to a question or voting questions and answers up or down.

The naming of the site is a bit of programmer-oriented humor, indicating that this site is where programmers go when their stack has overflowed, e.i. when theyÂ´re out of ideas and need help.
